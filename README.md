# TripCraft AI - Smart Travel Itinerary Generator

TripCraft AI is your personal AI travel planner that creates perfect itineraries in seconds, tailored to your dreams. Simply provide your destination, travel dates, interests, and budget, and our AI will generate a personalized travel plan.

## How to Use

1. Visit the homepage and fill out the travel form with your destination, dates, number of travelers, interests, and budget
2. Our AI will generate a personalized itinerary within seconds
3. Review and customize your itinerary as needed
4. Sign in to save your itinerary or share it with others
5. Export your itinerary as a PDF for offline access

## Project Structure

- `src/` - Main source code
  - `components/` - Reusable UI components
  - `pages/` - Page components for routing
  - `integrations/` - External service integrations (Supabase)
  - `hooks/` - Custom React hooks
- `supabase/` - Supabase functions and migrations

**Edit a file directly in GitHub**
